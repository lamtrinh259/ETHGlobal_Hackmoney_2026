About
Yellow Network lets you deposit crypto once and make lightning-fast transactions without waiting for blockchain confirmations or paying gas each time. It combines the best of both worlds ‚Äî the speed of off-chain systems and the security of smart contracts.
Most interactions between parties don't need immediate on-chain settlement. Consider a chess game with a 10 USDC wager:
- On-chain approach: Every move requires a transaction ‚Üí 40+ transactions ‚Üí $100s in fees
- State channel approach: Lock funds once, play off-chain, settle once ‚Üí 2 transactions ‚Üí minimal fees
State channels let you execute unlimited off-chain operations between on-chain checkpoints.
üí° How it works:
When you send funds or interact with an app, your transfers happen off-chain through a secure network of Yellow nodes that keep balances in sync. It feels instant, like using a regular web2 app. Smart contracts on each blockchain act as safes: they hold your deposit, verify everything, and make sure your money is protected. If you ever stop using the app or want to withdraw, your final balance is settled on-chain. Your session is protected by unique digital keys that only you control. No one can change your balance or take your funds. Even if something goes wrong, your assets automatically return to you.
üöÄ Key features:
- Works as a Layer-2 network, not a new blockchain
- Enables real-time, gasless payments that still settle safely on your preferred chain
- Designed for games, apps, and tools that need instant user actions and smooth UX
- Includes built-in liquidity pools to make sure there‚Äôs always capital available for fast transactions
- Compatible with all EVM chains, Solana and others coming soon.
